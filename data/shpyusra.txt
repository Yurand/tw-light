Yush Ranger

Primary Weapon:
  Shoots rainbow spears. The spears can be controlled while the primary weapon
  button is pressed by pressing the left or right buttons. The spears are not
  destroyed on contact but are stuck into the victim. If such a spear recieves
  damage the damage is instead given to the ship.
  
Special:
  Like colliding with a wall that is originated like the ship. Any lateral speed
  of the ship is flipped and multiplied by a factor depending on the current
  lateral speed.
