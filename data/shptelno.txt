Telluri Nova

Idea: Butch.

Main weapon: plasma shots; this drains some batt, and one level of the plasma shield. Plasma shots are intangible. They are launched in the direction of the enemy ship.

Special: plasma shield, having 5 energy levels and able to absorb 5 damage. Plasma shield is required for main to function. If the plasma shield comes into contact with an enemy ship, it deals some damage.
