The Ulzrak Interceptor is a small ship
with a bite.

fire - launches an intertia dampening
energy bolt.  This slows down any target
that it hits, in addition to doing damage.
cost is 1 power.

special - engages Zoom drive.  Cost is
4 power, and there is a startup delay.
When in zoom mode, the ship travels at
very high speeds, and will do 5 points
of damage to anything it impacts.
